A penetration test (pen test) is an authorized simulated 
attack performed on a computer system to evaluate its security.
 Penetration testers use the same tools, techniques, 
and processes as attackers to find and demonstrate the business impacts of weaknesses in a system.

white box testing:
tester is provided with the full information and then let him/her to test
the system;

black box testing: nothing is provided to the tester and let him/her to 
test the system.

gray box testing:
The tester has some know how of the system not the complete knowledge of th e
system and is allowed to test the system.
